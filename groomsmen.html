Michael Riordan

Groomsman

Some say the legend of Ryan and Michael's friendship began way back in-utero, while their mothers, already friends, did a stomach bump (like a chest bump between football players, only with their baby bumps) and declared "these boys will be best friends for life!" Forced together at a young age, the bounds of their friendship would know no borders. They've survived thick and thin, from childhood best friends, to college shenanigans and even roommates in their post graduate early careers. Sharing a strong bond, the heights of mischief they have achieved together would make frat boys blush, and are too sultry to share - tucked away into the deep recesses of their memories. But from time to time, as life wears on, they'll get together and revel in the stories of their youth, the hijinks survived, and ramble on about the adventures that lie ahead...
